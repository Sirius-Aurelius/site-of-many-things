## *Session Summary*

In **Session 12**, the DIADEMs were plunged deeper into the fragmented psyche of **Eleonora Humblewood**, witnessing a series of "selective" historical visions designed to prove her truth while mirroring the party’s own involvement with her legacy.

### **The Healer’s Burden (1325 AD)**

The journey began in the **Hogwarts Boathouse**, which had been converted into a makeshift **Plague hospital** . The party experienced this as floating **blue motes**, watching a younger, gifted version of Eleonora before her mind succumbed to madness .

- **Ancient Medicine:** Eleonora was seen pioneering cures by combining **Star Grass** with the raw power of **Ancient Magic crystals**—the same materials **Septimus Finch** and **Moira Thorne** would research centuries later.
- **The First Haunting:** You witnessed the exact moment the "blessed curse" took hold. As the dying began to cross the veil, the ghosts realized Eleonora could perceive them. They rushed toward her with a desperate hunger to be felt, marking the onset of the haunting that would eventually drive her to isolation.

### **The Great Fire & The Forbidden Cure (September 1666)**

The scene shifted to a smoke-filled basement in **Pudding Lane**, the apothecarium of **Bartemius Finch** .

- **The Escape:** As a braying mob surrounded the building, the party aided Bartemius—a small man and ancestor to the **Finch line**—as he scrambled through a window to escape the encroaching flames.
- **The Hidden Key:** You watched as he secured a **large, latrine-like treasure chest** (identical to the one found in the **Cold Plant Room**) with a keyhole hidden on the reverse. Most significantly, you saw him hide the key inside the hollowed-out base of a **Hufflepuff cup** .
- **The True Cause:** Eleonora revealed that the **Great Fire of London** was not a mere accident; it was sparked by an unknown power unleashing a **Giant Fire Crab** upon the city. She warned that throughout history, irresponsible powers have sought to harness the magic she and the Finches tried to protect .

### **The Industrial Blight (1851)**

The final vision brought you to the shadow of the **Crystal Palace**, where the authorities were attempting to mechanize the very power you have been investigating .

- **The Machine:** You saw a massive construct designed to capture and utilize **Ancient Magic** drawn from the earth.
- **The Failed DIADEMs:** A small group of "exceptional" students was seen attempting to create magical constructs. However, the magic was too volatile; the students lost control of their creations in a scene of chaotic violence .
- **The Death of Petunia Arbuthnot:** You witnessed the tragic death of a student, **Petunia Arbuthnot**, the specific casualty that forced the Ministry to shelf the **D.I.A.D.E.M. program** for nearly a century until **Minister Hector Fawley** revived it for you.
