## *Session Summary*
### AI-generated Recap
> Following the tremors of November 1939, the DIADEM students were placed on an **accelerated learning program**, with all three protagonists learning _Expelliarmus_ and _Engorgio/Reducio_. Individually, **Cassie** mastered _Slugulus Eructo_, _Relashio_, and _Pellucidi Pellis_, while **Basil** learned _Fumos_, _Immobilus_, and _Abscondi_, and **Vi** acquired _Protego Maxima_, _Finite Incantatem_, and _Reparo_. **Vi** sought advice from her mother regarding her misfiring magic, but received a cagey response, while **Basil received a signed cricket bat** from his father to replace the one he used against the kktchen pixies. **Cassie** received a request from her mother, **Lavender Weasley**, to act as a "Secret Santa" for the Muggle children of **Locharbor**, an act that would technically violate the International Statute of Secrecy.

> A significant portion of the session focused on **Bernard the Niffler**, whose persistent theft of Christmas ornaments led Cassie to develop a unique new spell: _**Objectum Revelio**_. Using this magic, the trio tracked Bernard to a hallway where he had been bribed by the **Hogwarts Kitchen Elves** with his own box of shiny baubles to prevent him from pilfering theirs. Following this levity, the atmosphere turned dark when Vi and Cassie pressed Basil for information about his mother; under blunt questioning, **Basil admitted that he had killed his mother**, Annabelle Featherstone. After a brief, distressed escape attempt, **Cassie and Basil made peace** over a cricket-bat-shaped chocolate before heading to the undercroft.

> The trio attended **Nearly Headless Nick's Festive Deathday Party**, where they encountered a blue mote of light believed to be the essence of **Eleonora Humblewood**. This encounter triggered **shared visions**: Cassie saw a young, vibrant Eleonora, while Basil saw her as an older student writing at length in a grimoire. The gathering was then interrupted by the manifestation of the **Yule Ghoul**, a featureless silver soldier in a "Tommy" silhouette that seemingly **transcends time**, appearing to the trio exactly as it had appeared to Eleonora centuries earlier.

> Before the party dissolved, Basil was gifted a **transfigured rubber duck** by the Yule Ghoul. The group also discovered a potential ancestral link, as they learned Eleonora’s Charms professor was **Felix Bourne**, a forebear of their current professor, **Elara Bourne**. The students finally left the undercroft for the deserted corridors, feeling **perturbed and unsettled** by the reappearances of these ancient entities.
