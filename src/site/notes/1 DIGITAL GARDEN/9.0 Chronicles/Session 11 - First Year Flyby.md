## *Session Summary*

#### AI Generated Recap
> We witnessed a pivotal chapter for the D.I.A.D.E.M. students, marked by heavy emotional revelations, a dangerous investigation into the **Hogsmeade sinkhole**, and the discovery of an ancient magic emitting device beneath the earth.
> 
> The session began in **Professor Dumbledore’s** Transfiguration class, where the students attempted **Inanimatus Conjurus** (the Conjuration Incantation). **Vi Thorne** successfully summoned an ornate perfume bottle, while **Cassie Weasley** conjured a one-handed wall clock—a sentimental object reminiscent of a gift from her father to her mother. However, the lesson turned tragic for **Basil Featherstone**; his attempt resulted in a singed and scorched version of his childhood companion, **Ruffles the Teddy**, prompting him to flee the classroom in distress.
> 
> Seeking a discrete location to discuss the truth behind **Annabelle Featherstone’s** death, the trio headed to the **Room of Requirement**. This time, the room was accessed via a revolving door of multi-coloured panes and manifested as a familiar sitting room from **Mallory Court**, complete with tea and biscuits.
> 
> In this private sanctuary, Basil finally recounted the full story of his mother's passing. He explained that in 1928, while suffering a night terror, he instinctively lashed out with magic at a looming figure—his mother—killing her instantly. During this heavy conversation, Cassie read Basil's tea leaves, noting that following the threads of **Eleonora Humblewood’s** story would yield positive short-term results but mixed long-term consequences.
> 
> The group continued their magical training, acquiring a range of new potions including **Herbicide Potion**, **Regerminating Potion**, and **Confusing Concoctions**. Amidst this, Vi discovered that **Penelope (Penny) Perkins** was acting erratically. Investigation revealed Penny was "wired" on a dangerous cocktail of **Baruffio's Brain Elixir** (to sharpen her mind) and **Wide-Eye Potion** (to prevent sleep). While Penny was taken to the **Hospital Wing** to recover, Cassie hid the surplus supply in **Agnes Finch’s** side table.
> 
> The narrative shifted to the **Hogsmeade sinkhole**, an enormous 100ft wide gap in the road. Intending to bypass the **Ministry of Magic** squad guarding the site, Cassie used a forged permission slip bearing a copy of **Percival Graves’** signature. Upon arrival, they witnessed a squad under **Alastair Thorpe-Wick** firing ineffectual spells into the void.
> 
> As they approached, the **blue mote of Eleonora Humblewood** appeared. Basil was suddenly pulled into the earth, with the mote orbiting him reassuringly as he plummeted. Cassie and Vi pursued on broomback; during the descent, Vi noted **Acromantula** moving toward the surface and found her magic misfiring due to the intense **Ancient Magic** emanating from the depths.
> 
> At the bottom of the sinkhole (over 300ft down), the trio discovered:
> - **The Clockwork Device:** A curious metal box filled with complex clockwork mechanisms. Using **Specialis Revelio**, Cassie discerned it was emitting the Ancient Magic that was buffeting Vi.
> - **The Passageways:** They found ancient tunnels leading in two directions. While one was visible to all, the eastern tunnel was hidden behind an **illusory shield** similar to those at the Forbidden Forest Test Pit—a shield Vi could see right through.
> 
> After contacting **Jackdaw Finch** via **Messaging Scrap**, they were warned that the **Clockwork Device** was a hugely powerful weapon or disruptor; acting on his advice, Cassie abandoned the device in a large chamber.
> 
> The mote led them to a circular chamber featuring a central pedestal and marble columns. The mote entered a glass orb on the pedestal, which Basil recognised from his visions. They discovered that moving the orb altered the room’s architecture, manifesting different doors depending on its height.
> 
> The trio eventually entered a hole in the ceiling and followed a long passage. Cassie attempted to manifest an exit, and a door appeared with the same "instant gratification" seen in the **Room of Requirement**. However, upon opening it, they found only a solid stone wall behind the door. The mote of Eleonora phased through the stone, leaving the party perplexed and trapped in the tunnel.
