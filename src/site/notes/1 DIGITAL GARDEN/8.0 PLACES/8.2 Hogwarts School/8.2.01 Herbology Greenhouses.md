# Description

- Large arched entrance into a central atrium where the professor's workstations can be found. 
- Smaller greenhouses surround the atrium on three sides (grand arched entrance on the 4th side). 
- Ancillary greenhouses used predominantly by upper years (OWL/NEWT level students) for their herbological experiments
