## Description

Goblin warriors are a specialized subset of goblins, historically and potentially contemporaneously functioning in roles of combat and defense.

#### Role and Expertise

* **Primary Role:** Combat and defense, protecting goblin interests.
* **Expertise:** Skilled in metalworking, crafting weapons and armor with unique magical properties.
* **Weapons and Armor:** Utilize goblin-made weaponry and armor.

#### Characteristics and Interactions

* **Loyalty:** Fiercely loyal to their kind and traditions.
* **Pride:** Strong sense of pride and tradition in their warrior role.
* **Historical Significance:** Involved in goblin rebellions and conflicts throughout wizarding history.
* **Appearance:** Armed and armored, displaying unique goblin craftsmanship.
