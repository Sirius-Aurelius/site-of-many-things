# Description

From player backstory:
>_Lavender, Cassie's mother, a witch with a warm heart and a knack for making ends meet, ran a small, illicit business from inside their tent: she was a Muggle-Wizard Liaison and Fixer. She helped newly-arrived Muggleborns navigate the wizarding world and often acted as an intermediary for wizards needing discreet assistance in the Muggle realm, earning barely enough to keep their large family fed._
