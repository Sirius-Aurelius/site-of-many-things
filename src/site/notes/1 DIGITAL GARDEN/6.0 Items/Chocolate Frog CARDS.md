*Collectible, common-rare*

## Description

This collection of **Chocolate Frog Cards** is based on the _Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets_ video game, which included 101 distinct witches and wizards, categorized into Bronze, Silver and Gold rarities.

## Bronze Cards (46 Total)

These cards represent widely known figures or those with significant historical achievements:

- [ ] **#1 Merlin:** A legendary British wizard from the Middle Ages.
    
- [ ] **#2 Cornelius Agrippa:** A German wizard and author of many magical works.
    
- [ ] **#4 Grogan Stump:** A popular British Minister for Magic (1811–1819).
    
- [ ] **#5 Gulliver Pokeby:** Magizoologist and author of Why I Didn't Die When the Augurey Cried.
    
- [ ] **#6 Glanmore Peakes:** A Scottish wizard famous for slaying the Sea Serpent of Cromer.
