*Potion, uncommon* 

## Description

When you drink this potion, you gain resistance to fire damage for 1 hour.

### Antidote to: 
None

## Recipe:

* 1 bursting mushroom
* 1 powdered wartcap
* 1 vial of salamander blood

**Flawed**:
The duration is 10 minutes, instead of 1 hour.

**Exceptional:** 
The duration is 2 hours, instead of 1 hour.
