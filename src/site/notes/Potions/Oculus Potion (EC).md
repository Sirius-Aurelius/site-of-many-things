*Potion, common* 

## Description
Drinking this deep orange potion removes the blind condition, restoring your eyesight to its normal state.

### Antidote to: 
- [[Spells/W&W Homebrew/Conjunctivia\|Conjunctivia]]

- 1 flask of pufferfish eyes
- 1 newt spleen
